<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Fire Safe Plywood Market is categorized into two types: UCFA and UCFB. UCFA refers to Under Compression Fire Assembly, while UCFB stands for Under Construction Fire Barrier. UCFA plywood is specifically designed to protect structural elements by delaying the spread of fire. It is used in the assembly of floor, ceiling, and roof systems. On the other hand, UCFB plywood acts as a barrier during construction, preventing the spread of fire to adjacent areas. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing fire safety in buildings.</p></p>
