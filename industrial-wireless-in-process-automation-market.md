<p><p>In the industrial wireless market, different types of wireless technologies are utilized for process automation purposes. Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN) refers to wireless networking within a limited area, such as a plant or facility. Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN) enables communication between devices within a short range, typically for personal use. Satellite (GNSS) enables wireless connectivity through satellite communication systems. Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN) utilizes low-power, long-range wireless communication for low-bandwidth applications. Cellular market involves wireless communication through cellular networks, allowing widespread connectivity for various industrial applications.</p></p>
