<p><p>Bio-based propylene glycol (PG) is a renewable alternative to traditional petroleum-based PG. It is derived from plant-based sources such as corn, sugarcane, or vegetable oils, making it more environmentally friendly. There are two types of bio-based PG available in the market: bio-based 1,2-propylene glycol and bio-based 1,3-propylene glycol. Bio-based 1,2-propylene glycol is commonly used in various industrial applications such as solvents, antifreeze, and cosmetics. On the other hand, bio-based 1,3-propylene glycol finds applications in the production of unsaturated polyester resins and functional fluids. Both types offer similar functionalities to their petroleum-based counterparts while reducing carbon emissions and dependence on fossil fuels.</p></p>
