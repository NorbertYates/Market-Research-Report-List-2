<p><p>Coal needle coke is a high-quality carbon material used in the production of graphite electrodes, which are an essential component in electric arc furnaces used in steel production. The increasing demand for steel and the growing steel industry will be the primary factors driving market growth. Graphite electrodes are crucial in the production of lithium-ion batteries, which are extensively used in electric vehicles. As the adoption of electric vehicles increases worldwide, the demand for graphite electrodes and, consequently, coal needle coke will show significant growth.</p><p>Additionally, the rapid industrialization and urbanization in emerging economies such as China and India will contribute to market expansion.
