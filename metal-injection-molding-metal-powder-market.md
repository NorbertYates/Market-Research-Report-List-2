<p><p>Metal injection molding (MIM) is a manufacturing process that combines the versatility of plastic injection molding with the high-strength properties of metal. It allows for high production volume, close tolerances, complex shapes, and reduced machining costs. It also provides the ability to manufacture parts with a wide range of materials, including stainless steel, titanium, cobalt-chrome, and copper alloys.</p><p>The global metal injection molding metal powder market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for small, lightweight, and high-performance metal components across various industries is one of the primary factors driving this growth. Industries such as automotive, aerospace, healthcare, and consumer electronics are increasingly adopting metal injection molding due to its ability to produce complex and high-strength parts.</p><p>Additionally, the rising focus on additive manufacturing and the development of advanced materials for metal injection molding are anticipated to fuel market growth.
