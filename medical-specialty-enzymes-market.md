<p><p>The Medical Specialty Enzymes market is classified into different types based on their functions. Oxidoreductases are enzymes that facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, involving the transfer of electrons between molecules. Transferases, on the other hand, aid in the transfer of functional groups, such as methyl or phosphate groups, between molecules. Lastly, the "Others" category includes enzymes with various functions, such as hydrolases that catalyze hydrolysis reactions or isomerases that facilitate isomerization reactions. Each of these enzyme types plays a crucial role in several biological processes and has specific applications in medical research and diagnostics.</p></p>
