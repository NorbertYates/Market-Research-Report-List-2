<p><p>The liquid chemical shipping market includes three types: deep-sea chemical tankers shipping, coastal chemical tankers shipping, and inland chemical tankers shipping. Deep-sea chemical tankers are designed to transport large quantities of liquid chemicals across oceans and seas. Coastal chemical tankers are used for shorter trips along the coastlines, delivering chemicals to nearby ports. Inland chemical tankers operate on rivers and inland waterways, transporting chemicals between inland terminals and industrial areas. Each market type has specific requirements and regulations to ensure safe and efficient transportation of liquid chemicals.</p></p>
