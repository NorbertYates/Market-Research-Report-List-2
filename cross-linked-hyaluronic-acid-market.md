<p><p>Cross-linked hyaluronic acid is a modified version of hyaluronic acid, a natural substance found in the human body. This modified version is commonly used in dermal fillers for facial rejuvenation, as it provides longer-lasting results compared to non-cross-linked hyaluronic acid fillers.</p><p>The current outlook of the cross-linked hyaluronic acid market is highly promising.
