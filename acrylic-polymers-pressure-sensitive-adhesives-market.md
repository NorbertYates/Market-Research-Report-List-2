<p><p>The acrylic polymers pressure sensitive adhesives market is composed of three main types: solvent type, emulsion type, and light curing type. Solvent type adhesives are formed by dissolving the adhesive in a solvent, creating a tacky and pressure-sensitive surface. Emulsion type adhesives, on the other hand, are formed by dispersing the adhesive in water. Finally, light curing type adhesives are cured by exposure to ultraviolet light, resulting in a strong and durable bond. These different types cater to various applications and offer a wide range of benefits to different industries.</p></p>
