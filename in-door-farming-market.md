<p><p>In the indoor farming market, there are various types of cultivation methods such as hydroponics, aeroponics, aquaponics, soil-based, hybrid, and others. Hydroponics involves growing plants without soil by using nutrient-rich water. Aeroponics is a method that suspends plant roots in air and mists them with a nutrient solution. Aquaponics combines fish farming with hydroponics, where fish waste provides nutrients for plant growth. Soil-based farming uses traditional soil as a medium for plant growth. Hybrid farming combines different methods to maximize efficiency and productivity. Other methods include vertical farming, container farming, and greenhouses with advanced technologies.</p></p>
