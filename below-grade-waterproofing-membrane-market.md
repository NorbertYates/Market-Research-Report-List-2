<p><p>The Below Grade Waterproofing Membrane market is categorized into different types based on the materials used. Hard Mortar, Flexible Mortar, Acrylic Ester, and One Component Polyurethane are four such types. Hard Mortar refers to a waterproofing membrane made from a mixture of cement and sand, providing a rigid and durable solution. Flexible Mortar is a similar membrane, but with added polymers to enhance flexibility. Acrylic Ester membranes are made from acrylic resins, offering good water resistance and UV stability. One Component Polyurethane membranes consist of a single-component polyurethane formulation, providing excellent waterproofing properties.</p></p>
