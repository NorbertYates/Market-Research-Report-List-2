Statistical arbitrage funds use statistical models to identify pricing inefficiencies, while convertible and fixed income arbitrage funds exploit discrepancies in convertible and fixed income securities. Commodity spread trades involve trading spreads between different commodities, and there are also other types of markets that quant funds may target based on their specific strategies and objectives.</p></p>
