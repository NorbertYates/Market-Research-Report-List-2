<p><p>The Modified Bitumen Membrane market consists of two main types: SBS Type and APP Type. SBS Type membranes are made from a mixture of asphalt and a synthetic rubber called Styrene Butadiene Styrene. These membranes offer enhanced flexibility, durability, and resistance to temperature changes, making them ideal for regions with extreme weather conditions. APP Type membranes, on the other hand, are made from a blend of asphalt and a plastic polymer called Atactic Polypropylene. These membranes provide excellent resistance to UV radiation and high temperatures, making them suitable for areas with intense sunlight and heat.</p></p>
