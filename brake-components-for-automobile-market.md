<p><p>The brake components market for automobiles consists of various types of products such as brake pads, brake shoes, brake lining, brake rotor, brake drum, and others. Brake pads are used to stop the vehicle by applying friction to the rotors. Brake shoes, on the other hand, function similarly but are used in drum brake systems. Brake lining refers to the material used to line the brake shoes or pads, enhancing their friction. Brake rotors are discs attached to the wheel, and when squeezed by the brake pads, they slow down the vehicle. Brake drums are used in drum brake systems and provide a surface for the brake shoes to press against. Other components in this market include brake calipers, brake hoses, and brake fluids, which are all crucial for efficient braking performance.</p></p>
