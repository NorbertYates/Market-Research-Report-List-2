<p><p>Metal bipolar plates are used in fuel cells and are responsible for conducting electricity between individual cells in a fuel cell stack. They are made of metal (such as stainless steel or titanium) and have parallel gas channels on both sides to distribute the reactant gases evenly across the fuel cell. Bipolar plates play a crucial role in the efficient performance of a fuel cell system by providing electrical conductivity, gas distribution, and structural support.</p><p>The future outlook for the metal bipolar plates market is promising.
