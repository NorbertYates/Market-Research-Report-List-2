<p><p>Rotational moulding equipment refers to machinery used in the rotational moulding process, which is a manufacturing technique for producing hollow plastic products. This process involves heating and rotating a moulded plastic material, usually in powder or liquid form, causing it to evenly coat the interior of the mould and solidify into the desired shape. The rotational moulding equipment includes rotational moulding machines, moulds, and auxiliary equipment such as ovens, cooling systems, and material supplies.</p><p>The future outlook of the rotational moulding equipment market is expected to be positive, driven by several factors.
