Centrifuges are used to separate solid particles from liquid solutions. Dryers and granulators help in the drying and granulation processes of pharmaceutical products. Other equipment includes fillers, mixers, mills, and sterilizers, which are essential in different stages of pharmaceutical processing. These machines play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and effectiveness of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
